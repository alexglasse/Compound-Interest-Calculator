For illustrative purposes only. All figures are hypothetical and nominal (not adjusted for inflation). The real purchasing power of any projected value will be lower than shown, depending on future inflation rates. Nothing on this calculator constitutes financial advice, investment advice, or a recommendation to buy, sell, or hold any asset.

Past performance is not a reliable indicator of future results. All investments carry risk, including the risk of losing some or all of the capital invested. The S&P 500 projection assumes a constant 10% nominal annual return compounded monthly — actual market returns vary significantly year to year and can be negative for extended periods. The Bitcoin power law model (Burger model: log₁₀(price) = 5.84 × log₁₀(days since genesis) − 17.01) is a long-run mathematical regression, not a forecast. Bitcoin is a highly volatile asset and can lose substantial value rapidly. No model can predict future prices. Monthly Bitcoin contributions are assumed to purchase at the power law fair value price, which will differ from actual market prices.
